About Us

Discover a laundry experience like no other at Access Laundry. Our laundromat offers a warm welcome, pleasant environment, & modern machines ensuring your laundry receives optimal care. Everyone is welcome & over the years we have met & got to know many local residents & people just passing through. We have welcomed a traveling marching band catching up on the laundry during a concert tour, youth sports teams from across the country in town for tournaments, & people visiting the area from overseas. As a veteran-owned & community-focused business, we proudly support first responders, veterans, & local organizations aiding those in recovery. Whether a local resident or visitor, we look forward to seeing you soon.

Our Machines

Deliberately old-school, our coin-operated system is reliable & eliminates transaction fees, credit card holds & unused balances on laundry cards. Our two change machines convert $1-$20 bills to quarters which are used to operate all machines. With six size options, our modern washers have been laid out with you, our customers, in mind. Large washers near the front door, smaller washers at the back, & 24 dryers. Many of our machines offer optional features such as pre-wash, extra rinse & extra high-speed spin, should you need them. From a small load to a king size comforter we have the machine you need in our clean & friendly environment.

Amenities

Our well-lit facility is staffed daily & is equipped with 24/7 security monitoring. Our separate male & female restrooms are cleaned regularly & the latter includes a baby change station. For those who need it we offer name-brand laundry products over the counter & in vending machines, plus a range of laundry bags. Our waiting area includes vending machines with a variety of snacks & drinks.

Comforter Care Service

We offer professional care for your comforters. Simply drop it off during counter hours, & we'll have it expertly cleaned & ready for you the next day. We'll notify you once it's ready.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring my comforter to wash?

Yes, we have large washers that can handle any machine-washable comforter up to king size. Or drop it off at the counter for next day service

Can I start my washer or dryer, leave & come back for it?

Certainly, all we ask is that you return to remove your laundry as soon as your machine completes its cycle

Do I need to bring my own laundry products?

You may bring your own detergent & softeners & for those that need it we offer a range of laundry products in-store

Are customer rest rooms available?

Our facility has separate male & female rest rooms, the latter includes a baby change station
