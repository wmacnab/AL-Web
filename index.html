About Us

Discover a laundry experience like no other at Access Laundry. Our laundromat offers state-of-the-art facilities, ensuring your clothes receive optimal care with top-notch washing and drying equipment. Embrace the convenience of finding quality laundry products over the counter and in vending machines. As a community-driven establishment, we proudly support first responders, veterans, and local organizations aiding those in recovery. Opt for the simplicity of our deliberately old-school, coin-operated system, ensuring a seamless process without transaction fees, credit card holds, or unused balances on laundry cards.

Our Machines

Elevate your laundry routine with Access Laundry's premium washing machines and dryers. Our cutting-edge equipment is designed to handle a variety of loads, ensuring your clothes receive the care they deserve. Experience efficiency and reliability with our 24-hour availability, allowing you to do your laundry on your schedule. Our state-of-the-art machines offer advanced features, delivering exceptional cleaning and drying results. From large-capacity washers to efficient dryers, Access Laundry provides the perfect solution for achieving laundry excellence in a convenient, modern, and customer-friendly environment.

Amenities

Access Laundry offers a comfortable and inviting atmosphere with a dedicated seating area, allowing you to relax while your laundry is in progress. Quench your thirst and satisfy your snack cravings with our conveniently located snack and drink machines. For added convenience, our change machine seamlessly transforms bills into quarters, ensuring you have the right currency for our coin-operated machines. Experience a pleasant environment year-round with our effective heating and air conditioning systems. Our laundromat is equipped with 24/7 security cameras, ensuring a safe and secure environment for you and your belongings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring my comforter to wash?

Yes, we have large washers that can handle any machine-washable comforter up to king size. Or drop it off at the counter for next day service

Can I start my washer or dryer, leave & come back for it?

Certainly, all we ask is that you return to remove your laundry as soon as your machine completes its cycle

Do I need to bring my own laundry products?

You may bring your own detergent & softeners & for those that need it we offer a range of laundry products in-store

Are customer rest rooms available?

Our facility has separate male & female rest rooms, the latter includes a baby change station
